Section 2: Short Answer Questions (10 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Sanskrit sounds.

Sanskrit sounds means Vowels and consonants pronounced with correct place and effort. Example: अ, आ, क, ख. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Supplementary reading.

Supplementary reading means Reading beyond textbook for wider language exposure. Example: पत्रिका, समाचार, बाल कहानी. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Poetry.

Poetry means Rhythmic literary expression of feeling and imagination. Example: कविता. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Sanskrit teaching.

Sanskrit teaching means Teaching pronunciation, vocabulary, sentence use and values. Example: शब्दरूप, धातुरूप. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Reading.

Reading means Accurate, clear and rhythmic oral reading. Example: श्लोक वाचन. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

6. Write a short note on Punctuation.

Punctuation means Marks that guide pause and meaning. Example: पूर्ण विराम, अल्प विराम. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

7. Write a short note on Story.

Story means Short narrative with plot, characters and message. Example: कहानी. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

8. Write a short note on Subhashita.

Subhashita means Moral Sanskrit verse. Example: विद्या ददाति विनयम्. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

9. Write a short note on Writing.

Writing means Correct Devanagari form, matra and spacing. Example: मात्रा और संयुक्ताक्षर. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

10. Write a short note on Paragraph.

Paragraph means Focused group of sentences on one idea. Example: मेरा विद्यालय. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.
