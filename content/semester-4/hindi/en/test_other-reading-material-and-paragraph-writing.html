Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Supplementary reading.

Supplementary reading means Reading beyond textbook for wider language exposure. Example: पत्रिका, समाचार, बाल कहानी. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Punctuation.

Punctuation means Marks that guide pause and meaning. Example: पूर्ण विराम, अल्प विराम. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Paragraph.

Paragraph means Focused group of sentences on one idea. Example: मेरा विद्यालय. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Main idea.

Main idea means Central thought of a paragraph. Example: मुख्य भाव. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Coherence.

Coherence means Logical flow of sentences. Example: क्रमबद्ध विचार. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.
