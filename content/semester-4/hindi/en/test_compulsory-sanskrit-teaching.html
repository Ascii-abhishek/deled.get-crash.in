Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Sanskrit teaching.

Sanskrit teaching means Teaching pronunciation, vocabulary, sentence use and values. Example: शब्दरूप, धातुरूप. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Subhashita.

Subhashita means Moral Sanskrit verse. Example: विद्या ददाति विनयम्. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Vocabulary.

Vocabulary means Useful words for reading and speaking. Example: फलम्, पुस्तकम्. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Word form.

Word form means Noun form changing by case and number. Example: रामः, रामम्. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Verb form.

Verb form means Root form changing by person, number and tense. Example: गच्छति, पठति. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.
