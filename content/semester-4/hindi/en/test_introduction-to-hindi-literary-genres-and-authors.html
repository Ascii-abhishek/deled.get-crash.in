Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Poetry.

Poetry means Rhythmic literary expression of feeling and imagination. Example: कविता. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Story.

Story means Short narrative with plot, characters and message. Example: कहानी. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Essay.

Essay means Organised prose discussion on a topic. Example: निबंध. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Biography.

Biography means Life story of another person. Example: जीवनी. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Letter.

Letter means Written communication with format and purpose. Example: पत्र. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.
