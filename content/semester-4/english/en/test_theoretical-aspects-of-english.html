Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Grammar Translation Method.

Grammar Translation Method means Teaching through rules, translation and mother tongue. Example: Translate sentences. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Direct Method.

Direct Method means Teaching English through English and situations. Example: Object-based speaking. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Drill Method.

Drill Method means Repeated oral practice for habits. Example: Substitution drill. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Structural Approach.

Structural Approach means Teaching graded sentence patterns. Example: This is a pen. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Communicative Approach.

Communicative Approach means Using language for real communication. Example: Pair conversation. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.
