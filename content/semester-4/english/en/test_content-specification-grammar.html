Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Grammar.

Grammar means System of rules for meaningful language. Example: Sentence formation. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Sentence.

Sentence means Group of words giving complete sense. Example: The child reads.. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Compound sentence.

Compound sentence means Two independent clauses joined together. Example: I came and I saw.. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Complex sentence.

Complex sentence means Main clause with subordinate clause. Example: I know that he is honest.. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Command and request.

Command and request means Functional sentence forms for classroom language. Example: Open the book; please sit down. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.
