Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Preposition.

Preposition means Word showing relation of noun or pronoun with another word. Example: in, on, under. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Conjunction.

3. Write a short note on Place preposition.

5. Write a short note on Coordinating conjunction.
