Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Tense.

Tense means Verb form showing time and state of action. Example: present, past, future. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Present tense.

Present tense means Action now, habit or general truth. Example: She reads.. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Past tense.

Past tense means Action completed or continuing in past. Example: She read.. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Future tense.

Future tense means Action expected later. Example: She will read.. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Aspect.

Aspect means Simple, continuous, perfect and perfect continuous form. Example: has read, is reading. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.
