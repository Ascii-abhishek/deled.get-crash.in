Classroom Connection

  • Begin with a familiar example from the learner’s home, school or local community.
  • Use pair work, oral practice, chart work and short written reflection.
  • Close the lesson with one application question so learners connect concept with behaviour.

One-minute Revision

  • Writing skill: Ability to express ideas clearly in written form.
  • Composition: Organised writing on a topic.
  • Picture description: Writing based on observation of an image.
  • Letter writing: Formal or informal written communication.
  • Form filling: Writing correct information in a given format.

Common Exam Lines

  • Define Writing skill with example.
  • Write short notes on Composition and Picture description.
  • Differentiate Writing skill and Composition.
  • Explain classroom use of Writing.
