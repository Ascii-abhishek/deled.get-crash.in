Reading and Writing in the Pure Form of Script

Script learning includes letter recognition, matras, punctuation, spacing, directionality, nasal sounds, and meaningful writing practice.

Exam Snapshot

Key term Meaning Example
Script System of written symbols used to represent language. Devanagari
Punctuation Marks that show pause, question, emotion, or structure. Full stop and question mark
Matra Vowel sign attached to consonants in Devanagari. कि, की, कु
Spacing Proper gap between words and lines. राम घर गया
Dictation Writing heard words or sentences correctly. Teacher says a sentence, child writes it

Key Points

  • Children need large-to-small movement practice before neat notebook writing.
  • Letter shape, direction, matra placement, spacing, and punctuation should be taught together with meaning.
  • Reading and writing should use familiar words, labels, names, and short messages.
  • Common confusions include similar letters, wrong matra placement, missing nasal marks, and no spacing.
  • Assessment should observe legibility, accuracy, speed, comprehension, and expression.
