Climate and Season and Natural Regions in India

India's climate, seasons, monsoon pattern, and natural regions influence life, agriculture, settlement, and culture.

Exam Snapshot

Key term Meaning Example
Weather Short-term condition of atmosphere at a place. Today's temperature and rain
Climate Long-term average weather pattern of a region. Hot desert climate
Monsoon Seasonal wind system bringing most rainfall to India. South-west monsoon
Natural region Area with similar relief, climate, vegetation, and life pattern. Himalayan region
Season Part of year with particular weather conditions. Winter, summer, rainy season

Key Points

  • Weather changes daily; climate is studied over many years.
  • India has broad seasons: cold weather, hot weather, south-west monsoon, and retreating monsoon.
  • The Himalayas, northern plains, desert, plateau, coastal plains, islands, and forests show regional diversity.
  • Monsoon affects crops, rivers, festivals, transport, water supply, and disasters such as floods and droughts.
  • Natural regions should be taught with maps, pictures, local weather records, and livelihood examples.

Concepts And Differences

Difference or link Exam-ready line
Point 1 Weather is immediate and local; climate is long-term and regional.
Point 2 Season is a time period; natural region is a geographic area.
