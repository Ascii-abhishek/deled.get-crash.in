Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Unity in diversity.

Unity in diversity means Living together with many languages, religions and cultures. Example: India as a plural society. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Communalism.

Communalism means Putting religious identity above citizenship. Example: Religious conflict. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Casteism.

Casteism means Discrimination based on caste. Example: Untouchability or exclusion. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Terrorism.

Terrorism means Use of violence to create fear for political aims. Example: Attack on civilians. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Panchsheel.

Panchsheel means Five principles of peaceful coexistence. Example: Mutual respect and non-interference. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

Section 3: Long Answer Questions (4 Questions)

1. Explain unity in diversity.
  • India has regional, linguistic, religious and cultural diversity but constitutional values bind citizens.
  • Social issues become dangerous when identity is used for hatred, exclusion or violence.
  • Terrorism attacks peace, democracy and human rights.
  • Communalism and casteism weaken national integration and equal citizenship.
2. Write causes and effects of communalism.
  • Social issues become dangerous when identity is used for hatred, exclusion or violence.
  • Terrorism attacks peace, democracy and human rights.
  • Communalism and casteism weaken national integration and equal citizenship.
  • NAM and Panchsheel represent peaceful foreign policy and respect for sovereignty.
3. Differentiate communalism and casteism.
  • Unity: Accepts difference and builds cooperation (Respectful participation)
  • Communalism: Creates suspicion between communities (Prejudice and conflict)
  • Casteism: Creates hierarchy inside society (Discrimination and inequality)
4. Write a note on Panchsheel.
  • Use festivals, food, local dialects and folk arts to discuss diversity respectfully.
  • Frame classroom rules against name-calling, caste comments and religious ridicule.
  • Use group projects where children from different backgrounds share roles equally.
  • End with an evaluation question and quick revision.
