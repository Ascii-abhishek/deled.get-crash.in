Uttar Pradesh-A Holistic View

A holistic view of Uttar Pradesh covers location, natural regions, rivers, agriculture, minerals, energy, trade, culture, and heritage.

Exam Snapshot

Key term Meaning Example
Doab Land between two rivers. Ganga-Yamuna Doab
Alluvial plain Fertile plain formed by river deposits. Gangetic plain
Agriculture Main occupation involving crop cultivation and allied activities. Wheat, rice, sugarcane
Cultural heritage Historical, religious, artistic, and social traditions of a region. Varanasi and Lucknow traditions
Energy resource Source used to produce power for homes, farms, and industry. Thermal and solar power

Key Points

  • Uttar Pradesh lies in the northern plains and has strong links with the Ganga-Yamuna river system.
  • Major regions include western plains, central plains, eastern plains, Bundelkhand, and Terai areas.
  • Agriculture is important; wheat, rice, sugarcane, pulses, oilseeds, and vegetables are significant.
  • Industries, trade, transport, pilgrimage, tourism, handicrafts, and cultural centres support the economy.
  • The state has rich heritage: ancient cities, freedom movement sites, languages, fairs, music, crafts, and monuments.

Concepts And Differences

Point 1 Physical features explain land and rivers; cultural features explain people, traditions, and heritage.
