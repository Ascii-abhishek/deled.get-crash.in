1. Write the short meaning of Agencies Working at National and State Level.

National and state agencies support curriculum, teacher education, policy, research, distance learning, and school quality. It can be explained through definition, purpose, and classroom use.

2. Write one difference related to NCERT and NCTE.

NCERT gives academic support; NCTE regulates teacher education standards.

3. Write two key points from the chapter.

NCERT supports national curriculum, textbooks, research, surveys, and teacher resources. NCTE frames norms and standards for teacher education institutions and programmes.
