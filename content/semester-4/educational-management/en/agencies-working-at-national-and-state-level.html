Agencies Working at National and State Level

National and state agencies support curriculum, teacher education, policy, research, distance learning, and school quality.

Exam Snapshot

Key term Meaning Example
NCERT National body for curriculum, textbooks, research, and teacher support. Textbooks and learning outcomes
NCTE Regulatory body for teacher education standards. Teacher education norms
NIEPA Institution working on educational planning and administration. Training in school leadership
IGNOU Open university supporting distance and lifelong learning. Distance teacher education resources
SCERT State-level academic body for curriculum, training, and material. State textbooks and teacher training

Key Points

  • NCERT supports national curriculum, textbooks, research, surveys, and teacher resources.
  • NCTE frames norms and standards for teacher education institutions and programmes.
  • NIEPA works on educational planning, management, leadership, and administration.
  • IGNOU and open schooling expand access through distance and flexible learning.
  • SCERT and state bodies adapt curriculum, prepare materials, conduct training, and support DIETs.

Concepts And Differences

Difference or link Exam-ready line
Point 1 NCERT gives academic support; NCTE regulates teacher education standards.
Point 2 National agencies frame broad direction; state agencies adapt it to local language and needs.
