One-minute Revision

  • Stimulus: Event that starts a response.
  • Response: Behaviour shown after stimulus.
  • Reinforcement: Consequence increasing desired behaviour.
  • Punishment: Consequence reducing behaviour but may create fear.
  • Behaviour modification: Planned change in behaviour using cues and reinforcement.
