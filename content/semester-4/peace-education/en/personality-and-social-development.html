  • Habit and Temperament are common short-answer areas.
  • Definitions must be written in simple words with one classroom example.
  • For D.El.Ed answers, add child-centred teaching use and value-based application.

  • Temperament: Natural style of emotional response.
  • Self awareness: Understanding own feelings, strengths and limits.
  • Social development: Learning to live and work with others.
