Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Write a short note on Personality.

Personality means Integrated pattern of behaviour, thought and emotion. Example: confidence and responsibility. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

2. Write a short note on Habit.

Habit means Repeated behaviour becoming regular. Example: punctuality. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

3. Write a short note on Temperament.

Temperament means Natural style of emotional response. Example: calm or active. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

4. Write a short note on Self awareness.

Self awareness means Understanding own feelings, strengths and limits. Example: reflection diary. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

5. Write a short note on Social development.

Social development means Learning to live and work with others. Example: cooperation. In exams, write its definition, use, and one classroom connection.

Section 3: Long Answer Questions (4 Questions)

1. Define Personality with example.

  • Personality gives the base idea of the chapter and should be linked with examples.
  • Habit and Temperament are common short-answer areas.

2. Write short notes on Habit and Temperament.

3. Differentiate Personality and Habit.
  • Personality: Integrated pattern of behaviour, thought and emotion (confidence and responsibility)
  • Habit: Repeated behaviour becoming regular (punctuality)
  • Temperament: Natural style of emotional response (calm or active)
4. Explain classroom use of Personality and Social Development.
  • Begin with a familiar example from the learner’s home, school or local community.
  • Use pair work, oral practice, chart work and short written reflection.
  • Close the lesson with one application question so learners connect concept with behaviour.
  • End with an evaluation question and quick revision.
