Safety and First Aid

This chapter explains basic safety habits and first aid responses for common school and home accidents.

Key Points

  • First aid is immediate help given before medical treatment.
  • Stay calm and call an adult or medical help when needed.
  • Clean small wounds with safe water and cover them.
  • Do not touch electric wires or switches with wet hands.
  • Keep emergency contact numbers easy to find.

Classroom Connection

Prepare a simple first-aid corner and discuss how students should respond during minor accidents.
