Electric Charge and Current

This chapter covers electric charge, conductors, insulators, and electric current through daily-life examples.

Key Points

  • Electric charge can be positive or negative.
  • Like charges repel and unlike charges attract.
  • Conductors allow electric current to pass easily.
  • Insulators do not allow current to pass easily.
  • Electric current is the flow of electric charge.

Classroom Connection

Discuss household examples such as wires, switches, plugs, rubber coverings, and batteries.
