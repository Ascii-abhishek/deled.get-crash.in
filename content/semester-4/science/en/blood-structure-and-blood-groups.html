Blood Structure and Blood Groups

This chapter introduces the main components of blood, blood groups, blood banks, and safe transfusion practices.

Key Points

  • Blood contains plasma, red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets.
  • Red blood cells carry oxygen.
  • White blood cells help protect the body from infection.
  • Platelets help in clotting.
  • Blood group matching is important before transfusion.
