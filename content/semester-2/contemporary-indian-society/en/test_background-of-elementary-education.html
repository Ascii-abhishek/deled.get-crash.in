Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the meaning of Background of Elementary Education.

Background of Elementary Education helps a learner understand key terms, social use, and classroom practice.

2. Explain the relation between Gurukul and Buddhist education.

Gurukul is the central idea, while Buddhist education is a related supporting idea/example.

3. Write two classroom examples related to this chapter.

One example may come from school work and another from home or community experience.

4. Write two differences between Ancient and Medieval.

Ancient: Moral and spiritual discipline; Medieval: Traditional and religious institutions.

5. Why is this topic important for a primary teacher?

A primary teacher connects learning with child language, experience, social context, values, and useful life situations.
