Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the meaning of Major Issues of Emerging Society 1.

Major Issues of Emerging Society 1 helps a learner understand key terms, social use, and classroom practice.

2. Explain the relation between Universalisation and Equal opportunity.

Universalisation is the central idea, while Equal opportunity is a related supporting idea/example.

3. Write two classroom examples related to this chapter.

One example may come from school work and another from home or community experience.

4. Write two differences between Enrolment and Retention.

Enrolment: Child enters school; Retention: Child continues in school.

5. Why is this topic important for a primary teacher?

A primary teacher connects learning with child language, experience, social context, values, and useful life situations.
