Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the meaning of Educational Thoughts.

Educational Thoughts helps a learner understand key terms, social use, and classroom practice.

2. Explain the relation between Idealism and Naturalism.

Idealism is the central idea, while Naturalism is a related supporting idea/example.

3. Write two classroom examples related to this chapter.

One example may come from school work and another from home or community experience.

4. Write two differences between Idealism and Naturalism.

Idealism: Values and self-realisation; Naturalism: Nature and child freedom.

5. Why is this topic important for a primary teacher?

A primary teacher connects learning with child language, experience, social context, values, and useful life situations.
