Key Points

  • The financial system mobilises savings and provides credit for agriculture, industry and services.
  • Inflation reduces purchasing power, so price stability is important for poor and fixed-income families.
  • Industrialisation creates jobs, increases production and supports infrastructure growth.
  • Cottage and small industries preserve skills, use local resources and provide rural employment.
  • Multinational companies bring capital and technology but also raise concerns about local competition and profit transfer.
