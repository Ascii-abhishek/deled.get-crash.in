Key Points

  • Agriculture supplies food, raw material, employment and demand for industrial goods.
  • Low productivity can result from small holdings, poor irrigation, credit problems and traditional methods.
  • Green Revolution improved foodgrain production but also created regional and environmental concerns.
  • Agricultural development supports poverty reduction, rural markets and national income.
  • Modern policy focuses on irrigation, storage, credit, crop insurance, soil health and market access.
