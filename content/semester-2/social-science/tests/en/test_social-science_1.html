Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write two important points about Foundation of First Empire in India.

Mauryan, Gupta, and Vardhan dynasties and the rise of early empires in India. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

2. Write two important points about India in Feudalism Period.

Feudal India, Rajput dynasties, and major South Indian dynasties. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

3. Write two important points about Islam and Sultanate Period in India.

Rise of Islam, Arab and Turk invasions, Delhi Sultanate, Bhakti movement, and regional kingdoms. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

4. Write two important points about Natural Powers and Lithosphere.

Natural powers, eclipses, earth spheres, internal structure of earth, and rocks. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

5. Write two important points about Different Land Factors.

Internal, deformation, accidental, and external forces that change land structure. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

6. Write two important points about Atmosphere and Hydrosphere.

Atmosphere, winds, cyclones, anticyclones, hydrosphere, waves, currents, and tides. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

7. Write two important points about Indian Constitution.

Constitution formation, citizenship, fundamental rights and duties, directive principles, and consumer awareness. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

8. Write two important points about Contribution of Agriculture in Indian Economy.

Indian agriculture, production, green revolution, economic development, and national agriculture policy. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

9. Write two important points about Indian Finance System and Industrialisation.

Currency, inflation, monetary policy, industrial development, cottage industries, and multinational companies. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.
