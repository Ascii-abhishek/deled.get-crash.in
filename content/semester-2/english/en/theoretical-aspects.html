Key Points

  • Primary English teaching should build confidence before perfection.
  • The bilingual approach helps comprehension, but English exposure and practice must increase gradually.
  • Dr West's method gives importance to reading, vocabulary control and silent reading.
  • Audio-video material supports listening, pronunciation and context-rich vocabulary.
  • Language games make repetition meaningful and reduce fear of making mistakes.
