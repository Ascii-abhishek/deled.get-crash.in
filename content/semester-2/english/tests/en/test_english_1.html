Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write two important points about Theoretical Aspects.

Approaches, methods, bilingual approach, audio-video method, and language games in English teaching. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

2. Write two important points about Classroom Teaching: Content.

English in daily life, greetings, alphabet recognition, and teaching of reading. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

3. Write two important points about Teaching of Poetry.

Aims, steps, importance, and recitation of rhymes and poems with rhythm and intonation. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

4. Write two important points about Teaching of Writing Skills.

Writing practice, handwriting, spelling, and methods for developing writing skills. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

5. Write two important points about Grammar I.

Grammar basics, sentences, punctuation, and determiners of noun. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

6. Write two important points about Conversational Classroom Instructions.

Conversation, classroom instructions, and self-introduction practice. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

7. Write two important points about Pronunciation.

English sounds, vowels, consonants, phonemes, syllables, stress, and reading simple sentences. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

8. Write two important points about Content Specifications.

Composition, viva-voce, description, narration, extempore, letter writing, vocabulary, and storytelling. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

9. Write two important points about Grammar II.

Noun, pronoun, adjective comparison, verb tenses, sentence transformation, voice, and common errors. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

10. Write two important points about Pronunciation and Lesson Planning.

Production of sounds, silent letters, reading aloud, intonation, modulation, and lesson-plan preparation. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.
