Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write two important points about Earth and Space.

Solar system, stars, eclipses, celestial bodies, life on earth, and space campaigns. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

2. Write two important points about Soil and Crops.

Soil, crops, agricultural methods, insecticides, soil erosion, and preservation. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

3. Write two important points about Work and Energy.

Work, energy, sources of energy, conservation, and transformation of energy. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

4. Write two important points about Simple Machines.

Types of simple machines and their uses in daily life. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

5. Write two important points about Structure of Organisms.

Cells, cell organelles, tissues, and organ systems in living organisms. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

6. Write two important points about Activities of Life.

Nutrition, respiration, excretion, sensitivity, photosynthesis, growth, reproduction, and transpiration. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

7. Write two important points about Organs and Functions of Human Body.

Major human body organs, skeletal system, muscles, teeth, digestion, and circulation. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

8. Write two important points about Food, Health and Disease.

Food, nutrients, balanced diet, sanitation, health, communicable diseases, and immunisation. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

9. Write two important points about Classification of Elements.

Atomic structure, modern classification of elements, valency, chemical reactions, and formulae. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

10. Write two important points about Changes Occurring in Surrounding.

Recurring and non-recurring changes, chemical and physical changes, and heat exchange. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.
