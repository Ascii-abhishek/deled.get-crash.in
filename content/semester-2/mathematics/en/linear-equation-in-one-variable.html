Linear Equation in One Variable

Revise linear equations of first degree in one variable, balancing, transposition, word problems, and checking solutions.

Key Points

  • Standard form is ax + b = c, where a is not zero.
  • Use inverse operations: addition cancels subtraction and multiplication cancels division.
  • Keep equality balanced; what you do to one side must be done to the other.
  • In transposition, sign or operation changes when a term crosses the equality sign.
  • For word problems, define variable first: Let the number be x.
  • Always verify the solution in the original equation.
