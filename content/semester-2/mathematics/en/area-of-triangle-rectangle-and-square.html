Area of Triangle, Rectangle and Square

Revise area formulae for rectangle, square, and triangle, base-height idea, grid method, unit writing, and classroom derivation.

Key Points

  • Rectangle and square formulae can be derived by counting unit squares.
  • Triangle area is half of a rectangle/parallelogram with same base and height.
  • Use perpendicular height, not necessarily the side drawn slanting.
  • For composite figures, divide into known shapes and add/subtract areas.
  • If all dimensions are in cm, area is in sq cm.
  • In teaching, paper folding shows why triangle area is half.
