Key Points

  • Add or subtract only like terms; unlike terms remain separate.
  • While subtracting an expression, change signs of every term being subtracted.
  • Remove innermost brackets first, then move outward.
  • Multiplication distributes over addition and subtraction: a(b+c)=ab+ac.
  • Keep variable powers unchanged in addition/subtraction of like terms.
  • Check signs carefully; most exam mistakes occur near negative brackets.
