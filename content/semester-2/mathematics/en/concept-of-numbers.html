Concept of Numbers

Revise number families, number line representation, place value, rational and irrational numbers, and how children build number sense.

Key Points

  • Zero is a whole number but not a natural number in the usual school convention.
  • Every natural number is whole, integer, rational, and real.
  • Every integer is rational because it can be written with denominator 1.
  • A number line helps compare positive, negative, fractional, and decimal numbers.
  • Place value explains the value of each digit according to its position.
  • Number sense grows through counting, grouping, estimating, comparing, and representing.
