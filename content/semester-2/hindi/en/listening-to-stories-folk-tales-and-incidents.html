Key Points

  • Story listening develops attention, imagination, vocabulary and emotional expression.
  • Folk tales connect language learning with local culture, oral tradition and social values.
  • The teacher should tell stories with voice modulation, gestures, pause and eye contact.
  • Retelling should be encouraged in children's own words, including home language support at early stages.
  • Assessment can include sequence cards, summary, alternate title, moral and role-play.
