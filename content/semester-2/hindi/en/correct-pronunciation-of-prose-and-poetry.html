Key Points

  • Correct pronunciation develops clarity, confidence and listening-speaking accuracy.
  • Prose reading needs proper pause, meaning groups and natural speed.
  • Poetry recitation needs rhythm, emotion, pronunciation and voice modulation.
  • Teacher should demonstrate first, then use choral, group and individual reading.
  • Common errors should be corrected gently through repetition, sound drills and peer support.
