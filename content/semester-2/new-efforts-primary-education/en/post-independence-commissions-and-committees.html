Post-Independence Commissions and Committees

Revise post-independence education commissions, national policies, Operation Blackboard background, Yashpal Committee, and their impact on elementary education.

Key Points

  • After independence, education was linked with democracy, social justice, national integration, and development.
  • Kothari Commission is often called the most important because it treated education as an instrument of national development.
  • NPE 1986 gave strong focus to universal elementary education and support services.
  • Operation Blackboard aimed to improve minimum facilities in primary schools.
  • Yashpal Committee criticised heavy bags, rote learning, and disconnected textbooks.
  • NCF 2005 encouraged child-centred, activity-based, inclusive, and meaningful learning.
