Programmes for Elementary Education 2

Revise SSA, NPEGEL, School Chalo, KGBV, ECCE, child labour projects, mid-day meal, scholarships, and incentives.

Key Points

  • SSA combined access, infrastructure, teacher appointment, training, community mobilisation, inclusive education, and quality improvement.
  • Girls’ education programmes reduce gender gaps by addressing distance, safety, poverty, household work, and social hesitation.
  • KGBV is important for girls who need residential support to continue upper-primary education.
  • Mid-day meal supports nutrition and attendance and can reduce caste barriers when managed sensitively.
  • ECCE creates readiness before Class 1 and reduces drop-out in early grades.
  • Incentives are useful only when teaching quality, safety, and regular attendance are also ensured.
