Section 2: Short Answer Questions (10 Questions)

1. Define Science and write one classroom or exam point.

Science: Systematic study of nature through observation, experiment, and reasoning. Based on evidence.

2. Define Pressure and write one classroom or exam point.

Pressure: Force per unit area. Formula: P = F/A.

3. Define Organ and write one classroom or exam point.

Organ: Body part made of tissues performing a specific function. Heart, leaf, eye are organs.

4. Define Microorganism and write one classroom or exam point.

Microorganism: Living organism too small to see with naked eye. Seen with microscope.

5. Define Natural resource and write one classroom or exam point.

Natural resource: Material or energy from nature useful to humans. Air, water, soil, minerals, forests.

6. Define Carbon and write one classroom or exam point.

Carbon: Non-metal element with atomic number 6. Basis of organic chemistry.

7. Define Non-communicable disease and write one classroom or exam point.

Non-communicable disease: Disease that does not spread by infection. Diabetes, hypertension, cancer.

8. Define Environment and write one classroom or exam point.

Environment: All living and non-living surroundings affecting life. Includes air, water, soil, organisms.

9. Define Heat and write one classroom or exam point.

Heat: Energy transferred due to temperature difference. Flows from hot to cold.

10. Define Technology and write one classroom or exam point.

Technology: Application of knowledge to design tools, processes, or systems. Solves human problems.
