Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Carbon and write one classroom or exam point.

Carbon: Non-metal element with atomic number 6. Basis of organic chemistry.

2. Define Tetravalency and write one classroom or exam point.

Tetravalency: Carbon forms four covalent bonds. Allows many compounds.

3. Define Catenation and write one classroom or exam point.

Catenation: Ability of carbon to bond with itself in chains/rings. Unique strength of C-C bond.

4. Define Allotrope and write one classroom or exam point.

Allotrope: Different forms of same element with different structures. Diamond, graphite, fullerene.

5. Define Hydrocarbon and write one classroom or exam point.

Hydrocarbon: Compound containing only carbon and hydrogen. Methane, ethane, benzene.
