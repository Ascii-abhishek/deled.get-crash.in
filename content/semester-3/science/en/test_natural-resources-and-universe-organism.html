Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Natural resource and write one classroom or exam point.

Natural resource: Material or energy from nature useful to humans. Air, water, soil, minerals, forests.

2. Define Renewable resource and write one classroom or exam point.

Renewable resource: Resource replenished naturally in short time if used wisely. Solar energy, wind, forests.

3. Define Non-renewable resource and write one classroom or exam point.

Non-renewable resource: Resource formed over very long time and limited in stock. Coal, petroleum, minerals.

4. Define Conservation and write one classroom or exam point.

Conservation: Careful protection and wise use of resources. Needed for future generations.

5. Define Biodiversity and write one classroom or exam point.

Biodiversity: Variety of plants, animals, and microorganisms. Maintains ecosystem balance.
