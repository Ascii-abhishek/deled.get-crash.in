Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Heat and write one classroom or exam point.

Heat: Energy transferred due to temperature difference. Flows from hot to cold.

2. Define Temperature and write one classroom or exam point.

Temperature: Measure of hotness or coldness. Measured by thermometer.

3. Define Conduction and write one classroom or exam point.

Conduction: Heat transfer through solids by particle contact. Metals are good conductors.

4. Define Convection and write one classroom or exam point.

Convection: Heat transfer by movement of fluid. Sea breeze, boiling water.

5. Define Radiation and write one classroom or exam point.

Radiation: Heat transfer without medium. Sun heats Earth by radiation.
