Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Non-communicable disease and write one classroom or exam point.

Non-communicable disease: Disease that does not spread by infection. Diabetes, hypertension, cancer.

2. Define Communicable disease and write one classroom or exam point.

Communicable disease: Disease spread by pathogen from one host to another. Cholera, malaria, TB.

3. Define Diabetes and write one classroom or exam point.

Diabetes: Condition with high blood sugar due to insulin problems. Diet, exercise, medicine help.

4. Define Hypertension and write one classroom or exam point.

Hypertension: Persistently high blood pressure. Risk for heart and kidney disease.

5. Define Cancer and write one classroom or exam point.

Cancer: Uncontrolled growth of abnormal cells. Early detection is important.
