Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Organ and write one classroom or exam point.

Organ: Body part made of tissues performing a specific function. Heart, leaf, eye are organs.

2. Define External organ and write one classroom or exam point.

External organ: Organ visible from outside. Eyes, ears, skin, limbs.

3. Define Internal organ and write one classroom or exam point.

Internal organ: Organ located inside body. Heart, lungs, stomach, brain.

4. Define Sense organ and write one classroom or exam point.

Sense organ: Organ that receives stimuli. Eye, ear, nose, tongue, skin.

5. Define Digestive system and write one classroom or exam point.

Digestive system: Organs that break food into usable nutrients. Mouth, stomach, intestine.
