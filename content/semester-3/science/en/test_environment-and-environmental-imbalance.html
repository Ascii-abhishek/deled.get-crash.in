Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Environment and write one classroom or exam point.

Environment: All living and non-living surroundings affecting life. Includes air, water, soil, organisms.

2. Define Ecosystem and write one classroom or exam point.

Ecosystem: Interaction of organisms with physical environment. Pond, forest, grassland.

3. Define Environmental balance and write one classroom or exam point.

Environmental balance: Stable relationship among organisms and resources. Food chains help maintain it.

4. Define Environmental imbalance and write one classroom or exam point.

Environmental imbalance: Disturbance in natural balance. Causes disasters and biodiversity loss.

5. Define Pollution and write one classroom or exam point.

Pollution: Addition of harmful substances to environment. Air, water, soil, noise pollution.
