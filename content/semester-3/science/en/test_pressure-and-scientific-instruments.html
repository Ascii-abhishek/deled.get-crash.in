Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Pressure and write one classroom or exam point.

Pressure: Force per unit area. Formula: P = F/A.

2. Define SI unit of pressure and write one classroom or exam point.

SI unit of pressure: Pascal, equal to one newton per square metre. Pa = N/m2.

3. Define Atmospheric pressure and write one classroom or exam point.

Atmospheric pressure: Pressure exerted by air around Earth. Measured by barometer.

4. Define Liquid pressure and write one classroom or exam point.

Liquid pressure: Pressure exerted by liquid due to depth and density. Increases with depth.

5. Define Hydraulic machine and write one classroom or exam point.

Hydraulic machine: Machine using pressure transmitted through liquid. Hydraulic lift, brakes.
