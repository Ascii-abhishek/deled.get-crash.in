Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Science and write one classroom or exam point.

Science: Systematic study of nature through observation, experiment, and reasoning. Based on evidence.

2. Define Technology and write one classroom or exam point.

Technology: Application of knowledge to design tools, processes, or systems. Solves human problems.

3. Define Scientific attitude and write one classroom or exam point.

Scientific attitude: Curiosity, objectivity, open-mindedness, and evidence-based thinking. Essential for citizenship.

4. Define Observation and write one classroom or exam point.

Observation: Careful use of senses or instruments to collect information. First step of inquiry.

5. Define Experiment and write one classroom or exam point.

Experiment: Planned activity to test an idea. Controls variables when possible.
