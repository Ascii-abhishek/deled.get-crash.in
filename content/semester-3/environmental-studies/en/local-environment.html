Local Environment

Environmental Studies becomes meaningful when connected with children's surroundings. The local environment includes home, school, plants, animals, markets, roads, water sources, festivals, and occupations.

Learning Activities

  • Nature walk in the school campus.
  • Draw a map from home to school.
  • Interview a community helper.
  • Observe water use at home.

Assessment

Use drawings, oral explanations, group charts, and observation notes to understand children's learning.
