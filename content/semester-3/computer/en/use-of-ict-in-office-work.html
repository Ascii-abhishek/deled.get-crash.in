Use of I.C.T. in Office Work

Office ICT improves documentation, calculation, presentation, databases, communication, storage, and cyber safety.

Exam Snapshot

Key term Meaning Example
Word processor Software for typing, editing, formatting, and printing documents. Notice, letter, report
Spreadsheet Software for tables, formulas, charts, and calculation. Attendance percentage
Presentation Slide-based visual communication of ideas. School meeting slides
Database Organised collection of records for search and update. Student record table
Cyber security Practices that protect devices, data, identity, and networks. Strong password and backup

Key Points

  • Word processing supports notices, letters, certificates, minutes, and reports.
  • Spreadsheets simplify marks, attendance, budgets, inventories, and charts.
  • Databases help maintain student, library, scholarship, and stock records.
  • Email, shared folders, and teleconferencing make official communication faster.
  • Backups, passwords, antivirus care, and careful downloading are essential office habits.

Concepts And Differences

Difference or link Exam-ready line
