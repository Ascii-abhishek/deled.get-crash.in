Section 2: Short Answer Questions (10 Questions)

1. Define CWSN and write one classroom or exam point.

CWSN: Learners needing additional educational support. Needs can be temporary or long-term.

2. Define TLM and write one classroom or exam point.

TLM: Material used to make learning concrete and active. Can be charts, models, cards, real objects.

3. Define Guidance and write one classroom or exam point.

Guidance: Organised help for making suitable choices. Preventive and developmental.

4. Define School counselling cell and write one classroom or exam point.

School counselling cell: School-level arrangement for learner support. May include head teacher and trained teacher.

5. Define Disability and write one classroom or exam point.

Disability: Long-term physical, sensory, intellectual, or mental condition causing barriers. Barrier-free environment is essential.

6. Define Multisensory teaching and write one classroom or exam point.

Multisensory teaching: Uses sight, sound, touch, movement, and speech. Helpful for diverse learners.

7. Define Counselling and write one classroom or exam point.

Counselling: Personal helping relationship for understanding and solving problems. Needs trust and confidentiality.

8. Define DIET and write one classroom or exam point.

DIET: District Institute of Education and Training. Supports teacher training and educational improvement.

9. Define Impairment and write one classroom or exam point.

Impairment: Problem in body function or structure. Disability grows when society creates barriers.

10. Define Universal Design for Learning and write one classroom or exam point.

Universal Design for Learning: Planning multiple ways to engage, present, and express learning. Benefits all learners.
