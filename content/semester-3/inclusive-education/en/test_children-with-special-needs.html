Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define CWSN and write one classroom or exam point.

CWSN: Learners needing additional educational support. Needs can be temporary or long-term.

2. Define Disability and write one classroom or exam point.

Disability: Long-term physical, sensory, intellectual, or mental condition causing barriers. Barrier-free environment is essential.

3. Define Impairment and write one classroom or exam point.

Impairment: Problem in body function or structure. Disability grows when society creates barriers.

4. Define Inclusive education and write one classroom or exam point.

Inclusive education: All children learn together with support. It changes school to fit child.

5. Define Individualised support and write one classroom or exam point.

Individualised support: Support planned according to learner need. May include IEP, aids, peer buddy.
