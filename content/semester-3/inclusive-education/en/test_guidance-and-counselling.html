Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Guidance and write one classroom or exam point.

Guidance: Organised help for making suitable choices. Preventive and developmental.

2. Define Counselling and write one classroom or exam point.

Counselling: Personal helping relationship for understanding and solving problems. Needs trust and confidentiality.

3. Define Adjustment and write one classroom or exam point.

Adjustment: Healthy balance between personal needs and environment. School transition often needs support.

4. Define Rapport and write one classroom or exam point.

Rapport: Warm relationship that makes child feel safe. First step in counselling.

5. Define Confidentiality and write one classroom or exam point.

Confidentiality: Keeping shared information private except safety risks. Builds trust.
