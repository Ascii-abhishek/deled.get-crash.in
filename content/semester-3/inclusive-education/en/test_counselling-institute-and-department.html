Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define School counselling cell and write one classroom or exam point.

School counselling cell: School-level arrangement for learner support. May include head teacher and trained teacher.

2. Define DIET and write one classroom or exam point.

DIET: District Institute of Education and Training. Supports teacher training and educational improvement.

3. Define Resource room and write one classroom or exam point.

Resource room: Space with special materials and support services. Useful for inclusive education.

4. Define Special educator and write one classroom or exam point.

Special educator: Teacher trained to support children with disabilities. Works with class teacher and parents.

5. Define Block Resource Centre and write one classroom or exam point.

Block Resource Centre: Local academic support unit for schools. Helps training and monitoring.
