Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Bank with an example.

Institution that accepts deposits, lends money, and supports payments.

2. Write the difference between Savings account and Current account.

Savings account: For personal saving. Current account: For business transactions.

3. Why is Fixed deposit important in this chapter?

Fixed deposit organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Set up a mock bank counter where learners fill deposit slips, update a passbook table, and calculate new balances.
