Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Set with an example.

Well-defined collection of objects.

2. Write the difference between Element and Roster method.

Roster method: Lists elements. Set-builder method: States property.

3. Why is Set-builder method important in this chapter?

Set-builder method organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Draw two circles for students who like cricket and football. Learners stand in regions to experience union, intersection, and complement.
