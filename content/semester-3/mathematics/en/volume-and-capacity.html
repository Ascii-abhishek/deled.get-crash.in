Core Points

  • Volume of cuboid = length x breadth x height.
  • Volume of cube = side x side x side.
  • 1 litre = 1000 millilitres and 1 cm^3 = 1 millilitre for water capacity activities.
  • Capacity is often measured for containers; volume can be for any solid.
  • Use consistent units before multiplying dimensions.
  • Irregular object volume can be estimated by immersing it in water and measuring displacement.
