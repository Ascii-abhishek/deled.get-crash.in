Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Base with an example.

The number or expression repeatedly multiplied.

2. Write the difference between Index and Exponential form.

Base: Quantity repeated. Index: Number of repetitions/power.

3. Why is Product law important in this chapter?

Product law organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Use folded paper or doubling charts to show powers of 2, then move to symbolic rules and error spotting.
