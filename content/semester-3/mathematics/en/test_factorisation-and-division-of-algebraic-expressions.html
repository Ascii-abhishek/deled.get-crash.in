Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Algebraic expression with an example.

Combination of constants, variables, and operations.

2. Write the difference between Term and Coefficient.

Expansion: Product to sum form. Factorisation: Sum form to product.

3. Why is Factor important in this chapter?

Factor organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Use rectangle area cards for x by x, x by 1, and 1 by 1 tiles. Learners arrange and read factors from side lengths.
