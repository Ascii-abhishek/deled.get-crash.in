Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Circle with an example.

Set of points at equal distance from a fixed centre.

2. Write the difference between Radius and Chord.

Segment: Bounded by chord and arc. Sector: Bounded by two radii and arc.

3. Why is Diameter important in this chapter?

Diameter organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Draw a large circle on chart paper. Learners label centre, radius, chord, arc, segment, and sector using coloured strips.
