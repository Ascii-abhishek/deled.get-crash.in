Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Ratio with an example.

Comparison of two quantities of the same kind by division, written a:b or a/b.

2. Write the difference between Antecedent and Consequent.

Ratio: Comparison of two quantities. Proportion: Equality of two ratios.

3. Why is Proportion important in this chapter?

Proportion organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Give groups of bottle caps in two colours. Learners form ratios, simplify them, then predict missing caps using proportion before writing symbolic solutions.
