Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Share with an example.

One unit of ownership capital of a company.

2. Write the difference between Face value and Market value.

Face value: Printed value. Market value: Actual trading value.

3. Why is Premium important in this chapter?

Premium organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Create paper shares with face value and market value. Learners compute dividend and compare yield for different purchase prices.
