Segment of Circle, Segment of Radius, Angle of Segment of Circle

This chapter explains circle parts: chord, arc, segment, sector, radius, central angle, angle in a segment, and related properties.

Core Points

  • Do not confuse segment with sector: segment uses chord; sector uses two radii.
  • A diameter divides a circle into two semicircles.
  • Equal chords subtend equal angles at the centre.
  • The angle in the same segment of a circle is equal.
  • Angle in a semicircle is 90 degrees.
  • Use paper folding, compass drawing, and thread to show radius, chord, arc, segment, and sector.

One-minute Revision

  • Circle: Set of points at equal distance from a fixed centre.
  • Radius: Line segment from centre to any point on circle.
  • Chord: Line segment joining two points on circle.
  • Diameter: Chord passing through centre; twice the radius.
  • Arc: Part of circumference between two points.
  • Segment: Region between a chord and its arc.
