Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Tangent with an example.

Line touching a circle at exactly one point.

2. Write the difference between Point of contact and Secant.

Tangent: Touches once. Secant: Cuts twice.

3. Why is External point important in this chapter?

External point organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Place a ruler around a drawn circle: outside, touching, and cutting. Learners record the number of common points in each case.
