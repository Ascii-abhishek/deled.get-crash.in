Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Biosphere with an example.

Zone of Earth where life exists.

2. Write the difference between Ecosystem and Natural region.

Biosphere: Life-supporting zone. Ecosystem: Functional interaction unit.

3. Why is Tundra important in this chapter?

Tundra organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Let groups design a natural-region poster showing climate, vegetation, animals, clothing, housing, and occupation.
