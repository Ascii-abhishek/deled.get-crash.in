Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Resource with an example.

Anything useful to humans and usable with knowledge and technology.

2. Write the difference between Natural resource and Human resource.

Renewable: Can be replenished. Non-renewable: Limited and slow to form.

3. Why is Renewable resource important in this chapter?

Renewable resource organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Students prepare a village or neighbourhood resource map and classify each item as natural, human-made, renewable, or non-renewable.
