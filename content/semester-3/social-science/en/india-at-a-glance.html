Core Points

  • India’s location gives it strategic importance in the Indian Ocean region.
  • The Himalayas influence climate, rivers, forests, and security.
  • The northern plains are fertile due to alluvial soil and river systems.
  • The peninsular plateau is rich in minerals and supports many industries.
  • Agriculture depends heavily on monsoon but irrigation reduces risk.
  • Use outline maps for physical divisions, rivers, crops, minerals, and industries.
