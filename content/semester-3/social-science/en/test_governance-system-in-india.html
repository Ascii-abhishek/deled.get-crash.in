Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Constitution with an example.

Supreme law that defines government and citizens’ rights.

2. Write the difference between Democracy and Legislature.

Legislature: Makes laws. Executive: Implements laws.

3. Why is Executive important in this chapter?

Executive organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Conduct a mock parliament on a school rule. Learners become speaker, ministers, opposition, and members, then track how a bill becomes a rule.
