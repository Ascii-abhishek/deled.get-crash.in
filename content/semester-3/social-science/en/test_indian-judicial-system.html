Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Judiciary with an example.

Organ of government that gives justice and interprets law.

2. Write the difference between Supreme Court and High Court.

Civil case: Private rights or compensation. Criminal case: Offence and punishment.

3. Why is District court important in this chapter?

District court organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Create a court pyramid and let learners place example disputes at the correct level: school fight, land dispute, rights issue, appeal.
