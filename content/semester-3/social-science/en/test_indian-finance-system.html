Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Public finance with an example.

Study of government income, expenditure, debt, and budgeting.

2. Write the difference between Budget and Revenue.

Direct tax: Paid directly by taxpayer. Indirect tax: Paid through price of goods/services.

3. Why is Expenditure important in this chapter?

Expenditure organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Let groups prepare a school picnic budget with expected income, expenditure, savings, and deficit-control suggestions.
