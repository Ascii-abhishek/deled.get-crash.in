Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Shivaji with an example.

Founder of strong Maratha power in western India.

2. Write the difference between Ashta Pradhan and Peshwa.

Maratha power: Regional Indian military-political power. European power: Commercial power becoming political.

3. Why is Chauth important in this chapter?

Chauth organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Use a map of India to mark Maratha centres and European trading posts, then discuss how trade routes became political routes.
