Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Babur with an example.

Founder of Mughal rule in India after the First Battle of Panipat.

2. Write the difference between Humayun and Akbar.

Akbar: Policy of broad alliance and religious tolerance. Aurangzeb: More orthodox policy and long Deccan wars.

3. Why is Mansabdari important in this chapter?

Mansabdari organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Ask learners to build a ruler timeline and place one administrative, cultural, and political feature under each major Mughal ruler.
