Core Points

  • The Mughal Empire began after Babur defeated Ibrahim Lodi in 1526.
  • Akbar’s policies of alliance, tolerance, and administration gave stability to the empire.
  • The mansabdari and zabti systems helped organise army, revenue, and officials.
  • Architecture, miniature painting, gardens, and Persian-Hindi cultural contact expanded.
  • Decline was gradual and caused by political, economic, military, and regional factors.
  • Teach with timelines, ruler cards, maps, and monument photographs.
