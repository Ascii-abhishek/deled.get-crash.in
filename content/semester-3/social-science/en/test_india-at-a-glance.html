Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Location with an example.

India lies in South Asia, mostly in the Northern and Eastern hemispheres.

2. Write the difference between Tropic of Cancer and Physical divisions.

Himalayan rivers: Perennial and long. Peninsular rivers: Mostly seasonal and shorter.

3. Why is Monsoon important in this chapter?

Monsoon organises the core idea and helps solve or explain questions correctly.

4. Write two common mistakes learners make in this chapter.

Two common mistakes are memorising definitions without examples and confusing similar terms.

5. Suggest one primary-classroom activity for this chapter.

Use blank India maps repeatedly: one day physical divisions, next day rivers, then crops, minerals, industries, and trade routes.
