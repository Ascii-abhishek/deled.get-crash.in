Core Points

  • Biosphere includes land, water, and air portions that support life.
  • Natural regions are shaped mainly by latitude, altitude, rainfall, temperature, and soil.
  • Human life styles vary with housing, clothing, food, occupation, and transport.
  • Dense population is often found where climate, soil, water, and transport are favourable.
  • Extreme climates such as deserts and tundra have sparse population.
  • Teach through region cards: climate, plants, animals, occupation, and settlement.
