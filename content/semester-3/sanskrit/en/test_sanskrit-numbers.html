Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Sanskrit Numbers.

This chapter revises Sanskrit numbers from one to fifty, their classroom use, and common forms.

2. Define Cardinal number and Ordinal number.

Cardinal number: A number that tells how many. Ordinal number: A number that tells order.

3. Write two classroom uses of Sanskrit Numbers.

Use attendance, objects, steps, and games to practise Sanskrit numbers orally and in writing. Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

It separates similar concepts clearly, so learners understand definition, example, and use without confusion.

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.
