Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Karak, Vibhakti and Signs.

This chapter explains karak relations, vibhakti forms, and common Hindi signs used to understand Sanskrit sentence roles.

2. Define Karak and Vibhakti.

Karak: Semantic relation of a noun with the verb. Vibhakti: Case ending that shows relation in the sentence.

3. Write two classroom uses of Karak, Vibhakti and Signs.

Ask learners to underline कर्ता, कर्म, करण in simple Sanskrit sentences and map them to Hindi signs. Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

It separates similar concepts clearly, so learners understand definition, example, and use without confusion.

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.
