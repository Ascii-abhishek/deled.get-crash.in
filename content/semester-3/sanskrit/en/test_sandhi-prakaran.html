Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Sandhi Prakaran.

This chapter revises Sanskrit sandhi as the joining of sounds with rules, types, and examples.

2. Define Sandhi and Sandhi-vicheda.

Sandhi: Change caused by joining two sounds. Sandhi-vicheda: Separating a sandhi word into original parts.

3. Write two classroom uses of Sandhi Prakaran.

Give word cards and let children join vowels or split simple joined forms aloud. Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

It separates similar concepts clearly, so learners understand definition, example, and use without confusion.

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.
