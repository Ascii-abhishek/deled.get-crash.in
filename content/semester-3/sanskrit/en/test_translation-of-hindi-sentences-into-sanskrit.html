Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Translation of Hindi Sentences into Sanskrit.

This chapter revises rules and practice for translating simple Hindi sentences into Sanskrit accurately.

2. Define Translation and Subject.

Translation: Changing meaning from one language into another. Subject: The doer or topic of a sentence.

3. Write two classroom uses of Translation of Hindi Sentences into Sanskrit.

Begin with subject-object-verb cards and translate simple daily sentences such as बालक पुस्तक पढ़ता है। Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

It separates similar concepts clearly, so learners understand definition, example, and use without confusion.

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.
