Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Copywriting, Transcription, Dictation and Simple Translation.

This chapter revises Sanskrit writing practice through neat writing, copying, transcription, dictation, and simple translation.

2. Define Copywriting and Transcription.

Copywriting: Neat copying of a given text with correct form. Transcription: Writing from a printed or written source accurately.

3. Write two classroom uses of Copywriting, Transcription, Dictation and Simple Translation.

Use a two-column board: Sanskrit sentence, Hindi meaning; then conduct a short dictation from familiar words. Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

It separates similar concepts clearly, so learners understand definition, example, and use without confusion.

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.

3. Explain the key terms and concepts with examples.

  • Transcription: Writing from a printed or written source accurately.
  • Dictation: Writing what is heard from the teacher.
  • Translation: Expressing meaning from one language into another.
  • Neatness: Clear, readable, well-spaced writing.
  • Accuracy: Correct spelling, form, and punctuation.
