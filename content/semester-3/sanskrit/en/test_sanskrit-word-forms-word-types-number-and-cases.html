Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Sanskrit Word Forms, Word Types, Number and Cases.

This chapter revises Sanskrit nouns, pronouns, gender, number, cases, and common shabda-rupa patterns.

2. Define Noun and Pronoun.

Noun: A naming word for person, place, thing, or idea. Pronoun: A word used in place of a noun.

3. Write two classroom uses of Sanskrit Word Forms, Word Types, Number and Cases.

Use a रूप तालिका for राम, फल, लता and ask learners to identify number and case. Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

It separates similar concepts clearly, so learners understand definition, example, and use without confusion.

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.
