Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Sanskrit Verb Forms.

This chapter revises Sanskrit roots, lakara, person, number, and common verb-form patterns.

2. Define Dhatu and Lakar.

Dhatu: A verbal root that carries basic action meaning. Lakar: Verb tense or mood marker in Sanskrit.

3. Write two classroom uses of Sanskrit Verb Forms.

Chant पठ् धातु forms in ताल and then use them in three simple sentences. Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

It separates similar concepts clearly, so learners understand definition, example, and use without confusion.

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.
