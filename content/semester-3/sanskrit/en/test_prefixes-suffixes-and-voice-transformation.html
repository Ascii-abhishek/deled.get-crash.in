Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Prefixes, Suffixes and Voice Transformation.

This chapter revises Sanskrit prefixes, suffixes, word formation, and voice transformation.

2. Define Prefix and Suffix.

Prefix: A meaningful element added before a root or word. Suffix: An element added after a root or word to form a new word/form.

3. Write two classroom uses of Prefixes, Suffixes and Voice Transformation.

Make word trees: root in the center, prefixes on the left, suffixes on the right, and new meanings below. Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.
