Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the main purpose of Sentence Construction Based on Dialogue Lessons.

This chapter explains how dialogue lessons help learners construct simple Sanskrit sentences for real communication.

2. Define Dialogue and Sentence construction.

Dialogue: Conversation between two or more persons. Sentence construction: Making meaningful sentences using words and rules.

3. Write two classroom uses of Sentence Construction Based on Dialogue Lessons.

Let pairs perform a two-line Sanskrit dialogue and then replace names, objects, and verbs to make new sentences. Oral practice, tables, pair work, and quick feedback can also be added.

4. Why is a comparison table useful in this chapter?

It separates similar concepts clearly, so learners understand definition, example, and use without confusion.

5. Write two suitable assessment methods for this chapter.

Oral questioning, short written exercises, matching tasks, form or word identification, classroom performance, and homework review are useful methods.
