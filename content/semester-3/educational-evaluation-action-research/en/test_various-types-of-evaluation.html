Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Diagnostic evaluation and write one classroom or exam point.

Diagnostic evaluation: Finds learning difficulties and causes before or during teaching. Used for remedial teaching.

2. Define Formative evaluation and write one classroom or exam point.

Formative evaluation: Checks learning during teaching for improvement. Low-stakes and frequent.

3. Define Summative evaluation and write one classroom or exam point.

Summative evaluation: Judges achievement at the end of unit or term. Used for grading/reporting.

4. Define Placement evaluation and write one classroom or exam point.

Placement evaluation: Finds entry level before instruction. Helps grouping and starting point.

5. Define Norm-referenced evaluation and write one classroom or exam point.

Norm-referenced evaluation: Compares learner with group performance. Rank and percentile are examples.
