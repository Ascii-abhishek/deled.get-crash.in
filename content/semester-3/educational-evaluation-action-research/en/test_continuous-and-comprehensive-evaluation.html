Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Continuous evaluation and write one classroom or exam point.

Continuous evaluation: Assessment spread across the teaching-learning period. It gives regular evidence.

2. Define Comprehensive evaluation and write one classroom or exam point.

Comprehensive evaluation: Evaluation of cognitive, affective, psychomotor, and social growth. It goes beyond marks in written tests.

3. Define Scholastic area and write one classroom or exam point.

Scholastic area: Subject learning such as language, math, EVS, science. Checked through tests, projects, oral work, notebooks.

4. Define Co-scholastic area and write one classroom or exam point.

Co-scholastic area: Life skills, attitudes, values, art, health, participation. Observation and rubrics are useful.

5. Define Formative assessment and write one classroom or exam point.

Formative assessment: Assessment during learning for improvement. Examples: quiz, peer check, oral response.
