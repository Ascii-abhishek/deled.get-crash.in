Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Research and write one classroom or exam point.

Research: Systematic and objective search for knowledge. Uses evidence and method.

2. Define Action research and write one classroom or exam point.

Action research: Small-scale inquiry by practitioner to improve own practice. Teacher is researcher.

3. Define Problem identification and write one classroom or exam point.

Problem identification: Selecting a real, specific, solvable classroom issue. Example: weak reading fluency.

4. Define Hypothesis and write one classroom or exam point.

Hypothesis: Tentative idea about possible solution or relation. In action research it is practical.

5. Define Action plan and write one classroom or exam point.

Action plan: Steps, resources, time, and expected outcomes for intervention. Must be manageable.
