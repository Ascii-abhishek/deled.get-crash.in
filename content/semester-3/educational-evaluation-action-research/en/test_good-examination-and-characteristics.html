Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Validity and write one classroom or exam point.

Validity: Degree to which a test measures intended learning. Most important quality of a test.

2. Define Reliability and write one classroom or exam point.

Reliability: Consistency of results across occasions or scorers. Clear items improve reliability.

3. Define Objectivity and write one classroom or exam point.

Objectivity: Scoring does not depend on personal bias. Marking scheme and MCQs help.

4. Define Comprehensiveness and write one classroom or exam point.

Comprehensiveness: Covers all important objectives and content areas. Use blueprint.

5. Define Discrimination and write one classroom or exam point.

Discrimination: Ability to distinguish high and low achievers. Good items are neither too easy nor too hard.
