Section 2: Short Answer Questions (5 Questions)

1. Define Innovation and write one classroom or exam point.

Innovation: Purposeful introduction of a new or improved practice. Must improve learning or access.

2. Define Educational technology and write one classroom or exam point.

Educational technology: Use of tools and processes to support learning. Can be low-tech or digital.

3. Define Activity-based learning and write one classroom or exam point.

Activity-based learning: Learning by doing, discussing, and reflecting. Useful in primary classes.

4. Define Child-centred pedagogy and write one classroom or exam point.

Child-centred pedagogy: Teaching planned around learner needs and participation. Teacher becomes facilitator.

5. Define ICT integration and write one classroom or exam point.

ICT integration: Meaningful use of digital resources in teaching-learning. Not just showing videos.
