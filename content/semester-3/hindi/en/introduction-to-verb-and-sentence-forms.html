Core Points

  • Teach verbs through action, picture, and sentence use.
  • Verb form changes with tense, person, number, and gender in Hindi.
  • Sentence forms include affirmative, negative, interrogative, imperative, and exclamatory.
  • Tatsam words are often formal; tadbhav and deshaj words are common in daily speech.
  • Transformation exercises improve grammar and expression together.
  • Use correction gently: ask children to compare forms and discover patterns.
