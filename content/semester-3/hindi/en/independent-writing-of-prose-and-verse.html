Core Points

  • Independent writing begins with oral discussion and vocabulary collection.
  • Prose writing needs sequence, clarity, facts, and paragraph unity.
  • Verse writing values feeling, rhythm, imagery, and compact expression.
  • Give children themes from school, family, nature, local festivals, and national days.
  • Correction should focus first on expression, then grammar and spelling.
  • Portfolio, display board, and reading aloud increase confidence.

Classroom Connection

Use a picture or school event and let children write first freely, then revise for sequence, vocabulary, and punctuation.
