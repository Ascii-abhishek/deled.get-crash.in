Section 2: Short Answer Questions (10 Questions)

6. Write the main purpose of Evaluation in Hindi.

This chapter revises evaluation of listening, speaking, reading, writing, grammar, literature, and classroom language outcomes in Hindi.

7. Define Poet and Author.

Poet: A creator who expresses feelings, imagination, and ideas in verse. Author: A creator who writes prose, stories, essays, plays, or other literary forms.

8. Define Context and Word meaning.

Context: The situation that gives a word or expression its exact meaning. Word meaning: The basic sense of a word used in a sentence.

9. Define Independent writing and Prose.

Independent writing: Writing one’s own ideas without copying a model. Prose: Ordinary sentence-based writing such as paragraph, story, or essay.

10. Define Verb and Transitive verb.

Verb: A word that shows action, state, or occurrence. Transitive verb: A verb that needs an object to complete meaning.
