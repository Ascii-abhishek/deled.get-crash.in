Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write the meaning of Internet and Networking.

Internet and Networking helps a learner understand key terms, life use, and classroom practice.

2. Explain the relation between Network and LAN.

Network is the central idea, while LAN is a related supporting idea/example.

3. Write two classroom examples related to this chapter.

One example may come from school work and another from home or community experience.

4. Write two differences between Download and Upload.

Download: Internet to computer; Upload: Computer to internet.

5. Why is this topic important for a primary teacher?

A primary teacher connects learning with child language, experience, activity, values, and useful life situations.
