Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write two important points about Meaning and Objectives of Teaching.

Meaning, definition, and major objectives of teaching. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

2. Write two important points about Communication.

Meaning, need, components, types, and classroom use of effective communication. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

3. Write two important points about Principles of Teaching.

Core principles that make classroom teaching purposeful and learner-centered. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

4. Write two important points about Maxims of Teaching.

Practical maxims such as known to unknown, simple to complex, and concrete to abstract. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

5. Write two important points about Teaching Techniques.

Common classroom techniques used to organize explanation, practice, and learner participation. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

6. Write two important points about New Methods of Teaching.

Modern approaches that support activity-based, participatory, and child-centered learning. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

7. Write two important points about Microteaching and Basic Teaching Skills.

Microteaching and basic skills such as introduction, questioning, explanation, reinforcement, and blackboard work. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

8. Write two important points about Expected Learning Levels.

Concept and use of expected learning levels and outcomes in planning and reinforcement. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

9. Write two important points about Teaching Learning Materials.

Types, features, preparation, use, maintenance, and precautions for teaching-learning materials. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

10. Write two important points about Meaning and Objectives of Teaching.

Meaning, definition, and major objectives of teaching. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.
