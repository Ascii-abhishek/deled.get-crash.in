Section 2: Short Answer Questions

1. Write two important points about Living Things.

Classification of natural and human-made things, and differences among living, non-living, plant, and animal forms. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

2. Write two important points about Plant Kingdom.

Plant parts, their functions, usefulness of plants and animals, and modification of plant parts. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

3. Write two important points about Reproduction in Plants.

Asexual and sexual reproduction, flower parts, pollination, fertilization, seeds, and seed dispersal. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

4. Write two important points about Physical Measurement.

Need and methods of measurement, standard systems, SI units, and common measuring instruments. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

5. Write two important points about Motion and Force.

Meaning, laws and types of motion, speed, and common types of force. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

6. Write two important points about Matter and Its States.

States of matter, properties and structure, dissolving, mixtures, and separation of mixtures. In an exam answer, add one classroom example and one teaching precaution.

7. Write two important points about Model Preparation.
