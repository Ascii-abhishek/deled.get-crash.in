Learning Environment

A learning environment includes the physical classroom, emotional safety, teaching materials, language use, peer relationships, and the teacher's attitude. Young children learn better when they feel respected and included.

What It Contains

  • Safe seating and movement space.
  • Charts, local objects, books, and low-cost learning materials.
  • Opportunities to speak, draw, ask, read, play, and explore.
  • Positive routines that help children know what to expect.

Teacher Role

The teacher creates a calm classroom by listening carefully, giving clear instructions, and encouraging children without fear.
